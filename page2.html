Arctic Foxes

My Favorites

I love arctic foxes! However they are on the endangered species list as climate change is melting the ice in the arctic and therefore the foxes are unable to get the food they need to survive and repopulate.

Hunting

When they are hunting, they can track the movements of mice and other rodents under the snow by feeling the vibrations. Then, once the mouse stops moving, the arctic fox jumps up in the air before coming back down and pouncing on its prey to stun them.
